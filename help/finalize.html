Finalize

The Finalize tab allows you to complete and publish a Slatepack transaction. Once finalized, the transaction is broadcast to the MWC blockchain, your coins are sent, and the operation cannot be reversed.
Please double-check the transaction amount and recipient address before confirming finalization and submitting.

To finalize successfully, your wallet must be connected to a healthy node. A node is considered healthy if it is online and connected to enough peers, ensuring that your transaction will propagate to mining pool nodes.

Node connectivity indicators are displayed at the bottom of this page. A green indicator means your wallet is properly connected to the MWC network.
