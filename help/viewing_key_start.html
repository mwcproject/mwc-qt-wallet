Viewing Key

This page allows you to perform operations with your Viewing Key. The Viewing Key enables scanning the blockchain to view all outputs associated with your wallet across all accounts.
Important: Sharing this key makes your transaction history visible, compromising the privacy of your funds. However, the Viewing Key does not permit spending the funds.

  • 'Show My Viewing Key' reveals the viewing key for this wallet. It can be used to view outputs but not to spend them.
  • 'Generate Ownership Proof' creates a message with signatures from the public keys associated with the Viewing Key, your wallet’s Slatepack/Tor address, and MWC MQS address. You can choose what information to include in the message. 'View Ownership Proof' validates the ownership proof and displays details about the included Viewing Key and addresses. Note: Generating an ownership proof shares your data — please be aware of the privacy implications.

Please keep in mind that ownership proof guarantees control of the private keys, but it does not guarantee that the Viewing Key and addresses belong to the same wallet. Additionally, wallet addresses can be updated at any time on the 'Listeners' page.

  • 'Scan blockchain with Viewing Key' allows you to view funds for any Viewing Key. Simply paste the key and click 'Start Scanning' to begin the process.
