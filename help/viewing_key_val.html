Validate Ownership Proof

Paste the ownership proof or load it by clicking the 'Load from File' button. Once uploaded, click 'Validate' to proceed.

If the proof is valid, the next screen will display the Network, Message, Viewing Key, Slatepack/Tor address, and MWCMQS address.

Please note, ownership proof confirms control of the private keys, but it does not guarantee that the Viewing Key and addresses are from the same wallet. Additionally, wallet addresses can be updated anytime on the 'Listeners' page.
