Transactions

Swap allows you to sell your MWC coins in exchange of other supported coins. Currently BTC and BCH are the suupported coin types.

You will need the MWC address of the other party with whom you are swapping coins to initiate swap. Please specify the rate at which you're willing to trade to proceed, and ensure sending enough funds to cover the transaction fee to avoid getting the swap canceled due to insufficient funds.
