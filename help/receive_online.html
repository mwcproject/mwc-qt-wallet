Receive

The Receive page lets you accept funds. Your MWCMQS, Tor, and Slatepack addresses are displayed prominently. Other users can use these addresses to send MWC to you.
Note: In the wallet settings you can configure which methods are allowed for receiving MWC.

If you enable online protocols (MWCMQS or Tor), your wallet must be online at the time the funds are sent. You can also select which account the funds should be deposited into.

Connection indicators show the current status of your Tor and MWCMQS listeners, helping you confirm whether your wallet is online and ready to receive funds.
