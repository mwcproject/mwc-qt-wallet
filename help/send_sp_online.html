Send coins

MWC Qt Wallet supports several ways to send funds. They fall into two categories:

  1. Online address-based sending.
  2. Offline Slatepack-based sending.

Several address types are supported.

Online sending methods include MWCMQS and Tor communication methods. MWCMQS uses community MQS hosts for message exchange. Tor leverages the Tor network. If Tor in your region is blocked, wallet will automatically switch to bridge connection.

Slatepack-based sending is an interactive 3-step process where the sender initiates a transaction and sends a Slatepack to the recipient. The recipient generates a response that is sent back to the sender. The sender then finalizes the transaction. While this is the most advanced method of sending, it is also the most private.
