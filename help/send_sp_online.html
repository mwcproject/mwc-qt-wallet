Send coins

MWC-QT-Wallet supports several ways to send funds. They fall into two categories:

  1. Online address based sending.
  2. Offline Slatepack based sending.

There are several types of addresses that are supported.

Online sending methods include MWC MQS and Tor communication methods. MWC MQS using community MQS hosts for message exchange. Tor is leverage the Tor network. In some countries Tor might be blocked and required bridge to work.

Slatepack based sending is an interactive 3 step process where the sender initiates a transaction, sends a Slatepack to the recipient. The receiver receives the slatepack, generates a response which is sent back to the sender. The sender then finalizes the transaction. While this is the most advanced method of sending, it is also the most private.
