The Listener page lets you check the status of the underlying wallet listeners. The green light indicates connected and the red light indicates disconnected.

The most important function of this page is to obtain a new MWCMQS address. It is recommended that you create a new receiving address each time you accept funds.

To do this, click the 'stop' button next to MWCMQS, then click 'next address'. Alternatively, you can go to a specific index by clicking on 'To index'.
