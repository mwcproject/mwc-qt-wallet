Swap allows you to sell your MWC coins in exchange for other supported coins. Currently supported coins are BTC, BCH, LTC, ZCash, Dash, Doge.

You will need the other party's MWCMQS or Slatepack/Tor address to initiate a swap. Please specify the rate you're willing to trade at, and ensure you send enough funds to cover the transaction fee to avoid the swap being canceled due to insufficient funds.
