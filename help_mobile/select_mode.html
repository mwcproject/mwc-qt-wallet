This page lets you choose whether you are running the MWC Qt Wallet as a standard online hot wallet (the default choice for most users), an "Online Node" which is used to submit transactions from cold storage, or a "Cold Wallet" which is used in conjunction with the "Online Node" and installed on a computer that never connects to the internet. Transactions are sent, received, and finalized on the "Cold Wallet" but submitted with the "Online Node"

More details about Cold Wallet use cases can be found in our Cold Wallet Guide
https://github.com/mwcproject/mwc-qt-wallet/blob/2019_09_cold_wallet/DOC/cold_wallet.md
